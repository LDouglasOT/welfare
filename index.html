Our Services

We provide holistic, person-centered care addressing physical, emotional, social, and spiritual well-being for every senior in our community.

Clinical & Nursing Care

24-hour nursing supervision, medication management and monitoring, health assessments and chronic disease support, assistance with personal care (bathing, dressing, grooming).

Daily Living Support

Nutritious meals and dietary support, housekeeping and laundry services, assistance with mobility and daily routines, safe, homelike environment.

Wellness & Engagement

Physical exercises and fall-prevention programs, memory and cognitive stimulation activities, social, recreational, and educational programs, emotional support and companionship.

Spiritual & Emotional Care

Pastoral visits and spiritual guidance, prayer and reflection spaces, support for residents of all faiths.

Transportation & Family Support

Transport to medical appointments and hospital visits, coordination with healthcare providers, regular updates and communication with families.

Our Care Approach

We follow a person-centered model, creating individualized care plans for each senior. Families and volunteers are partners in care, helping seniors achieve independence, wellness, and joy in daily life.

About Memory of Madia Care Foundation

A faith-inspired non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of older adults in Uganda.

Who We Are

Memory of Madia Care Foundation is a faith-inspired non-profit dedicated to improving the lives of older adults. We provide residential and home-based care to seniors, ensuring they live with safety, comfort, and a strong sense of community regardless of financial means.

Our Vision

To be a trusted leader in non-profit elder care, recognized for quality, integrity, and community impact, where every senior feels valued, safe, and respected.

Our Living Environment

Private or shared rooms with a homelike atmosphere. Safe, clean, and peaceful surroundings. Community areas for social interaction and leisure. Outdoor spaces for relaxation, gardening, and reflection.
