Our Story & Mission

Caring for Seniors with Compassion

Graceful Aging Foundation is a faith-inspired non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of older adults in Uganda. Based in Ntinda, Nakawa Division, we provide residential and home-based care to seniors, ensuring they live with safety, comfort, and a strong sense of community regardless of their financial means.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, person-centered care that enhances quality of life for seniors, empowering them to live with dignity and independence.

Our Vision

To be a trusted leader in non-profit elder care, recognized for quality, integrity, and community impact, where every senior feels valued, safe, and respected.

Our Core Values

These principles guide every interaction and care plan we develop for our residents.

Compassion

Caring for every resident with kindness and empathy.

Dignity

Respecting individuality and the wealth of life experience.

Integrity

Transparent, accountable, and trustworthy in all we do.

Community

Engaging families, volunteers, and partners in care.
