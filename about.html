Our Story & Mission

Memory of Madia

Care Foundation

Founded in faith and rooted in community, we have dedicated our work to honoring the lives of elders across Uganda — ensuring no senior faces old age alone, forgotten, or without dignity.

Caring for Seniors
with Compassion

Memory of Madia Care Foundation is a faith-inspired non-profit dedicated to improving the lives of older adults in Uganda. Based in Ntinda, Nakawa Division, we provide residential and home-based care to seniors, ensuring they live with safety, comfort, and a strong sense of community regardless of their financial means.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, person-centered care that enhances quality of life for seniors, empowering them to live with dignity and independence.

Our Vision

To be a trusted leader in non-profit elder care, recognized for quality, integrity, and community impact, where every senior feels valued, safe, and respected.

Our Core Values

These principles guide every interaction and care plan we develop for our residents.

Compassion

Caring for every resident with genuine kindness, warmth, and deep empathy.

Dignity

Respecting each individual's identity and the wealth of their life experience.

Integrity

Transparent, accountable, and trustworthy in every action we take.

Community

Engaging families, volunteers, and partners in every aspect of care.
