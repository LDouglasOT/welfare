About Us

Welcome to Graceful Aging Foundation

[cite: 1]

Graceful Aging Foundation is a faith-inspired non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of older adults. [cite: 5] We provide residential and home-based care to seniors, ensuring they live with safety, comfort, and a strong sense of community regardless of financial means. [cite: 6]

We focus on holistic care: physical, emotional, social, and spiritual well-being. [cite: 7] Our person-centered model involves creating individualized care plans where families and volunteers act as partners in care. [cite: 43, 44]

Our Mission
